Liberating math literature by simplifying creation and unrestricting publishing possibilities.

Creation is simplified by removing all obstacles except communicating mathematics.
* The following content can easily be created:
	* Text
	* Question (with numerical or literal answer)
	* Graph
	* Image
* The content are put into pages
* The pages are put into a book

Publishing is unrestricted by making all content accessible by everyone.
* The created books are put up on an open website
* All books and pages can easily be printed
